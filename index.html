Entertainment Law

We are often asked, "what's it take to become an entertainment lawyer." The short answer is experience as an attorney AND an intimate knowledge of the entertainment business, from its customs and practices to its language and lingo. At it's heart, entertainment law is really a combination of intellectual property and contract practice, but to to be truly effective on behalf of your client you must understand the ins and outs of the industry. Decades of experience in both the business and law of entertainment afford us that unique perspective.
